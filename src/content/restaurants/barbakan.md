A Manchester institution that has been doing this since 1964. The bread alone is worth the trip. Polish-influenced deli counter, excellent sandwiches, and the kind of calm, purposeful atmosphere that you don't find in places trying to be anything other than what they are. Buy the sourdough. Buy two.
