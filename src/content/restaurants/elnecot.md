Named after an old English word for a hamlet, Elnecot takes its sense of place seriously. The cooking is modern British with real conviction — seasonal ingredients, smart technique, and a drinks list that rewards the adventurous. The cocktail bar downstairs is one of Manchester's best kept secrets.
